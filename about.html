Our System Architecture & How it Helps You

We believe in high-performance engineering designed for merchant outcomes. Here is how our system is built to grow your shop safely.

Dual-Shield Secure Gateway

How it helps: Replaces laggy bot services with a stable, Facebook-certified connection. This guarantees 100% of customer messages are received and replied to instantly, even during peak sales campaigns.

Private Semantic Catalog

How it helps: Unlike third-party website builders that leak your margins, Kontext AI hosts database logs directly on your private server, matching spelling typos and Banglish variations in under 1 millisecond.

Cultural AI Personalization Brain

How it helps: Instantly greets customers politely with culturally natural greetings ("ভাইয়া" or "আপু") based on their name. It acts as an expert salesperson, negotiating prices and sealing deals within safety limits you set.

Automated Cashier & Anti-Fraud Shield

How it helps: Automatically checks bKash and Nagad payment receipts by cross-referencing incoming transaction logs in under 2 seconds. This completely eliminates invoice fraud and screenshot manipulation.

Sovereign Override & One-Tap Learning

How it helps: Forwards complex queries directly to your private Telegram bot. Replying in Telegram routes the message to the customer, silences the bot for 30 minutes to let you chat, and teaches the database automatically.

Ultra-Low Latency & High Margins

How it helps: Smart local caching minimizes AI token usage, saving you up to 90% in monthly API bills while maintaining instant response times.

1-Click Docker Containerization

How it helps: Bundles PostgreSQL, Redis, FastAPI app services, and Arq worker threads in isolated Docker containers, enabling 1-click deployments in under 5 minutes on any VPS.

Moderation Queue & JWT Security

How it helps: Extracted Q&A pairs are held in a secure Moderation Queue dashboard for admin approval. Access endpoints are locked with Bcrypt credentials and stateful JWT token validation.

Enterprise-Grade Reliability & Security Features

Designed to be resilient, cost-efficient, and secure against spam, ensuring your store is always open.

Frustration Detector & Priority Alerts

How it helps: Senses if a customer gets angry and routes the alert to Telegram. It automatically filters out duplicate events from network retries, preventing double-alerts in your admin chat.

Self-Healing API Failover

How it helps: Switches to local matching if AI APIs hit quotas. It automatically translates Bengali digits (০-৯) in phone numbers and cash amounts to standard formats, protecting your order database from format errors.

Anti-Spam & API Shield

How it helps: Caps inputs at 2,000 characters and verifies digital signatures to block malicious spam. It automatically truncates very long chat logs to guarantee you always receive your Telegram notifications.

Zero-Cost Local Greetings

How it helps: Answers standard inputs (like "Hi" or "Assalamu Alaikum") instantly in under 0.05 seconds via local rule checks, bypassing AI API calls completely to save you tokens and lower monthly operating fees.
