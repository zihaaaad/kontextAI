Step 2

Smart Telegram Alerts & Autotraining

If a customer asks a question that isn't in your catalog, the bot doesn't make up answers. Instead, it securely alerts your Telegram.

  • Instant Notifications: See customer inquiries on Facebook or WhatsApp directly in your Telegram chat.
  • One-Tap Training: Reply directly to the Telegram alert. The bot delivers the message to the customer AND automatically saves the Q&A and its vector embedding to the database catalog.
  • Agent Silence Window: Once you manually reply, the bot pauses for 30 minutes, allowing you to chat with the customer in real-time.
Step 3

Instant Checkout & Payment Verification

No more manually copying addresses or waiting to double-check bank transactions. Kontext AI extracts order details and auto-verifies bKash/Nagad transfers instantly.

  • Automated Shipping Details: Parses customer messages to extract names, 11-digit phone numbers, and addresses (district/thana) into structured lists.
  • MFS Payment Auto-Verification: Auto-reconciles transactions by matching customer Transaction IDs (TrxID) against parsed payment logs in under 2 seconds.
  • Anti-Fraud Protection: Blocks users attempting to confirm orders using fake or edited payment screenshots.
